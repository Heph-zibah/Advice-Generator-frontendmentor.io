The designs were created to the following widths:

- Mobile: 375px
- Desktop: 1440px

## Colors

- Light Cyan(`.card-body`): hsl(193, 38%, 86%)
- Neon Green(`.card-head`): hsl(150, 100%, 66%)

### Neutral

- Grayish Blue: hsl(217, 19%, 38%)
- Dark Grayish Blue(`.card`): hsl(217, 19%, 24%)
